CONSERVATION

As part of the Natior'Ala Association based in Madagascar, I conducted an ethnoprimatology study in communities in the western part of the Marotandrano Special Reserve, as well as a lemur survey in a few sites within the national park. I am grateful for the strong teamwork within our association, which played a crucial role in the success of this project.
